# Lead Research Assistant

## Activation
When the user asks to find potential customers, build prospect lists, identify target accounts, or prepare for business development.

## Workflow

### 1. Understand the Product/Service
- If in a code directory, analyze the codebase to understand the product
- Identify key features, benefits, and value proposition
- Understand what problems it solves
- Clarify the go-to-market strategy

### 2. Define Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)
- Target industries and sectors
- Company size ranges (employees, revenue)
- Geographic preferences
- Relevant pain points
- Technology requirements and stack
- Budget indicators

### 3. Research and Identify Leads
- Search for companies matching criteria
- Look for need signals: job postings, tech stack, recent news
- Growth indicators: funding, expansion, hiring
- Complementary products/services
- Competitive landscape

### 4. Prioritize and Score (1-10)

Consider factors:
- Alignment with ICP
- Signals of immediate need
- Budget availability
- Competitive landscape
- Timing indicators

### 5. Deliver Actionable Output

For each lead:

```markdown
## Lead: [Company Name]

**Website**: [URL]
**Priority Score**: [X/10]
**Industry**: [Industry]
**Size**: [Employee count/revenue range]

**Why They're a Good Fit**:
[2-3 specific reasons based on their business]

**Target Decision Maker**: [Role/Title]
**LinkedIn**: [URL if available]

**Value Proposition for Them**:
[Specific benefit for this company]

**Outreach Strategy**:
[Personalized approach — mention specific pain points, recent news]

**Conversation Starters**:
- [Specific point 1]
- [Specific point 2]
```

### 6. Summary Format

```markdown
# Lead Research Results

## Summary
- Total leads found: [X]
- High priority (8-10): [X]
- Medium priority (5-7): [X]
- Average fit score: [X]

## Leads
[Detailed entries for each lead]
```

### 7. Next Steps
- Save results to CSV for CRM import
- Draft personalized outreach messages
- Recommend prioritization based on timing
- Deep-dive research for top leads

## Tips for Best Results
- Be specific about your product and unique value
- Run from your codebase for automatic context
- Provide context about your ICP
- Specify constraints: industry, location, company size
- Request follow-up research on promising leads
